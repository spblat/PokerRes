This app helps us coordinate our weekly poker games. Here's how to use it:

  • Enter the password to gain access to the app. Good job, you did it!
  • Once inside, you can see the upcoming poker game date.
  • Check and see if the venue is available this week. If you're sure the wrong button is checked, feel free to fix it.
  • Reserve seats by adding player name(s). If you're not sure you're coming, please use an "alternate" slot.
  • Even if the venue is not marked as available, feel free to express interest by adding names. Just be sure it's on before you show up :-)
  • Click "Save" to submit your updates.
